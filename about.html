Your Satisfaction Is Our Priority


Rosy's houses cleaning services was born in August 2016 with the sole intention of helping busy families, and individuals regain their valuable time so they can focus on what they love the most. We are very grateful to have the opportunity to help others regain their personal time. Life is simply too short, and we must use our time wisely. Maintaining a clean home and office is necessary and a very important factor in all of our lives. We, at Rosy's House Cleaning, believe that by maintaining our home and office clean, it keeps us balanced. Our homes are our haven, a refuge from this stressful world. Our ancestors honored the home as a sacred space which provided protection and peace: we can reawaken this sense of life in your home, and turn it into a special place which can calm the mind and soothe the soul. Our mission is to clean your home or office, making it feel and smell wonderful, all while you spend your time on what you enjoy doing most. We value commitment to high quality service, respect for what is not ours, but treat it as if it is, dedication to training, safety and development, and sensitivity to the environment.
